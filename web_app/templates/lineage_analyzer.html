How Risk Score Acceleration is Calculated

Our methodology uses county-weighted competitor benchmarking to detect systematic risk score acceleration that may indicate potential gaming or fraud in Medicare Advantage risk adjustment.

1. Geographic Footprint Analysis

For each plan-year, we identify all counties where the plan operates and track enrollment in each county.

2. Competitor Benchmark Calculation

We calculate a county-weighted average of competitor risk scores in the same geographic markets, excluding the plan being analyzed.

3. Performance vs Competitors

We measure how much higher or lower the plan's risk score is compared to its county-weighted competitor benchmark.

4. Acceleration Detection

We use linear regression across multiple years to detect systematic acceleration in performance vs competitors over time.

5. Statistical Reliability

R-squared values indicate how well the linear trend fits the data, with higher values suggesting more systematic acceleration.

6. Contract/Plan Evolution

Plans may change contract numbers or plan IDs over time due to mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring, which we track through persistent lineage IDs.
